## 16. APPENDICES

### 16.1 Glossary of Terms

**Market Capitalization:** Total market value of a company's outstanding shares (Price × Shares Outstanding)

**P/E Ratio:** Price-to-Earnings ratio (Price per share / Earnings per share). Lower is generally better for value.

**PBV:** Price-to-Book Value ratio (Price per share / Book value per share). <1 indicates potentially undervalued.

**EPS:** Earnings Per Share (Net income / Shares outstanding). Higher is better.

**Yield:** 1-Year Yield (Annual return percentage). Higher is better for income investors.

**52-Week High/Low:** Highest and lowest prices over past 52 weeks.

**Volume:** Number of shares traded. Higher volume indicates better liquidity.

**Circuit Breaker:** Trading halt mechanism. In NEPSE, stocks can move ±10% in a day.
